--Overview--
ClothesToHome is an iOS app that allows users to organize personal items, item ownership, and item locations.
THis will help people looking to keep better track of their belongings within one home or homes across the country/world.
Flying to a family member's house for a visit and can't remember if you left a pair of boots there? Keep track with Clothes2Home and allow yourself to pack light.
Wondering if you still have that heirloom holiday decoration in your basement somewhere? Keep a digital log of your analog storage system to simplify your life.

--Core Features--
-AddOwner allows additions of different individuals whose items you wish to organize and keep track of.
-AddLocation allows a user to save the locations their items are distributed amongst.
-AddItem allows users to create a digital log of every item they own, and associate the item with an owner and location.
